Meal Prep

You will get tired of eating the same thing after a day or two. A better idea is to prep individual ingredients. Are you making beef and broccoli, make some extra beef to add to salads, omlettes, or other dishes throughout the week. Are you making some tacos? make a big batch of salsa for the week and reuse that beef you already have cooked. You get the idea, just don't go crazy. Stick prepping one or two things you know you will eat.

Here are some good foods to prep.
  • Chicken (really any meat). Just cook and shred it. Goes in tacos, stir fry, salad, sandwich, etc.
  • Pickles. Pickles last forever. Onions, cucumbers, and peppers are my favorite
  • Salad dressing. Making dressing is time consuming and it goes bad pretty fast but a home made caesar is so much better than the shelf brand shit.
  • Marinated meat. You really only need to marinade your meat for an hour but overnight doesn't hurt. Simple is better.
