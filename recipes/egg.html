Boiled

  1. Plop your eggs in a pan and cover fully with water.
  2. Bring pot to a boil.
  3. Turn off the heat and wait 10 minutes.
  4. Run eggs under cool water to stop cooking.
  5. Smacc egg with spoon. Use spoon to peel.
