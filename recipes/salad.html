Salad

Put green things in bowl, add sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Veggie base: cabbage, brussel sprouts, spring mix, bean sprouts, broccoli, romaine lettuce, spinach, kayle
  • Protein: salmon, chicken, (boiled) egg, tofu, tempeh (no one likes tempeh)
  • Toppings: onion, avocado, raw nuts and seeds (pumpkin, chia, walnut, etc..), cheese, carrot, cucumber, beans in moderation, tomato, olive, fruit
  • Dressing: anything by Primal Kitchen, oil and vinegar, Goddess, or nothing if you are a punk.
  • Banned: basically every salad dressing ever, deli meat, iceberg, corn, cranberries, dried fruit, peas, crutons, crunchy noodles, tortilla, pasta (you get the idea)

note: Most dressing is very dangerous, make sure to read the label. The man wants you to think ranch is healthy.
note: Bacon and tuna can probably be eaten a few times a month.
